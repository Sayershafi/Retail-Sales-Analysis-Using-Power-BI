<h1 align="center">Interactive Sales Dashboard – Power BI Project</h1>

Interactive Sales Dashboard built using Power BI to analyze global revenue, orders, profit, and customer trends through data-driven insights.

---

## Executive Summary

I developed an Interactive Sales Power BI dashboard to provide a comprehensive overview of business performance. The dashboard analyzes key business metrics such as revenue, profit, total orders, costs, returns, and quantity sold.

It allows stakeholders to understand global sales performance, identify profitable products, analyze customer demographics, and monitor monthly sales trends.

The dashboard highlights key metrics including **$24.91M total revenue**, **$10.46M total profit**, **25.16K orders**, and **84.17K quantity sold**, helping businesses make better data-driven decisions.

---

## Business Problem

Businesses generate large volumes of sales data across different regions, customers, and product categories. Without proper data visualization, it becomes difficult to analyze performance trends and identify key growth opportunities.

Decision makers need an analytical dashboard that answers questions such as:

- Which products generate the highest profit?
- How do revenue and profit change across months?
- Which countries generate the highest revenue?
- What customer segments drive the most profit?

Without a centralized dashboard, analyzing these insights becomes complex and time-consuming.

# Key Insights

- Strong revenue performance with **$24.91M total revenue**
- High profitability with **$10.46M profit**
- Certain product categories generate significantly higher profits
- Monthly sales trends reveal fluctuations across the year
- Revenue distribution across gender is nearly balanced
- Global sales activity spans multiple regions and countries
